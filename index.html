My journey to becoming a lawyer began with a passion for economics. As an Economics major at the University of California, Riverside (UCR), I was fascinated by the forces that drive financial systems and market dynamics. I excelled in my studies, developing a deep understanding of how economics shapes society. However, as I delved deeper into the field, I realized that my true passion went beyond analyzing numbers and theories—I wanted to make a tangible difference in people’s lives through advocacy. During my time at UCR, I became increasingly aware of the crucial role legal frameworks play in shaping economic outcomes and ensuring justice. This realization sparked my interest in law, where I saw an opportunity to blend my analytical skills with my desire to champion fairness and equity. Motivated by this new direction, I decided to broaden my academic pursuits to include law, embarking on a path that would allow me to combine my love for economics with a newfound passion for legal advocacy. Shifting my focus, I immersed myself in pre-law courses, where my background in economics provided me with a unique perspective. My ability to analyze data and assess regulatory impacts set me apart from my peers, giving me a broader understanding of the economic implications of legal issues. This transition from economics to law was driven by my relentless curiosity and a commitment to using my skills to effect positive change. At UCR, I distinguished myself by actively participating in mock trials and debate teams, where I honed my advocacy skills. My internships at Clutch Sports further reinforced my commitment to pursuing a legal career. My economics background proved invaluable as I navigated complex financial cases, while my growing legal expertise enabled me to advocate effectively for clients. Today, as a law student, I bring a unique blend of skills that bridges the worlds of economics and law. My journey reflects a deep commitment to justice and a desire to make a meaningful impact in the legal field. I continue to build on my experiences at UCR, approaching every challenge with the same dedication and integrity that have defined my academic and professional pursuits. As I prepare to enter the legal profession, I remain focused on using my diverse skill set to advocate for those who need it most. My journey from economics to law is a testament to my adaptability, perseverance, and unwavering belief in the power of law to create a more just society.
